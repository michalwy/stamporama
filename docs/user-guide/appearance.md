# Appearance

Stamporama supports light and dark themes.

## Theme modes

- **Light** — always uses the light theme.
- **Dark** — always uses the dark theme.
- **Auto** — follows your operating system's appearance setting. When your OS switches between light and dark mode, Stamporama switches with it automatically.

## Changing the theme

Use the theme toggle to switch between Light, Dark, and Auto. On collection pages, the toggle is in the sidebar. On the collections list page, it is in the top header bar.

Your choice is saved in the browser and persists across sessions. It does not require an account setting — each browser remembers its own preference independently.
